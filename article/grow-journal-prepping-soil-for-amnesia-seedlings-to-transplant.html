Grow Journal - Prepping soil for Amnesia seedlings to transplant

Created At：2023-09-06 08:45 Updated At：2023-09-06 21:40

Yesterday I realized I had a piece of wood laying around that was the perfect width for the shelf slot on my Costco shed. I cut it to the right length, and bam I have a gardening shelf in my bike shed!

I took the old dirt and cleaned out the old plants from it.

I then mixed in a half trowel of earthworm castings as fertilizer to recharge the soil. I’ll be using nutrients too so I think we will be fine recycling the dirt.

We then planted our little seedlings!
